About Us

Vision

♦ To establish the Department as a leading hub for innovation and entrepreneurship, fostering a culture of creativity, research excellence, and technological advancement. We aim to shape students into skilled professionals, innovators, and researchers, driving progress in the field of Computer Engineering and beyond.

Mission

♦ To provide a dynamic and collaborative learning environment that encourages innovative thinking, hands-on research, and creative problem-solving among students and faculty.

♦ To equip students with the technical expertise and professional skills needed to develop cutting-edge software solutions and drive innovation across industries.

♦ To strengthen industry-academia collaboration, enhancing students' exposure to real-world challenges and preparing them for leadership roles in the tech industry.

♦ To instill a strong sense of social responsibility, ethics, and sustainable practices in our graduates, ensuring they contribute meaningfully to society and the global economy.

Objective

♦ To cultivate a vibrant innovation ecosystem within I2IT that encourages students and faculty to explore creative ideas and entrepreneurial opportunities.

♦ To conduct innovation and entrepreneurship-focused activities in alignment with the guidelines provided by the Ministry of Education (formerly MHRD).

♦ To organize seminars, workshops, and expert talks on Intellectual Property Rights (IPR), startup culture, and entrepreneurial journeys, inspiring students to pursue entrepreneurial ventures.

♦ To promote student participation in innovation-driven activities like project/idea competitions and make them aware of government initiatives that support student entrepreneurs.

Functions

♦ To serve as a platform for organizing hackathons, innovation challenges, and ideation contests that promote student engagement in research and entrepreneurship.

♦ To facilitate mentorship from industry professionals and business leaders, providing students with valuable insights and guidance in nurturing their innovative ideas.

♦ To support students and faculty in securing seed funding from various agencies for developing prototypes and scaling innovative projects.

♦ To create awareness about Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) and assist students in protecting and commercializing their innovations.

♦ To bridge the gap between academia and industry, ensuring students gain hands-on experience and exposure to current trends and challenges in the tech industry.

About Us

IIC, I2IT Pune aims to induce an entrepreneurial mindset into the students and air an innovative streak in them. We are here to water the ‘Ideas’ in the bud and help them bloom into impactful endeavors through networking student enterprises from campus to incubators, seeding funds and angel investors to transform the newly proposed ideas into successful start-ups.
